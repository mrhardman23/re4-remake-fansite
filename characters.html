Basic Character Biographies

Leon S. Kennedy
The main protaganist of Resident Evil 4 Remake. Leon is a secret service agent working for the president to save his kidnapped daughter.
Ashley Graham
The secondary protaganist of Resident Evil 4 Remake. She begins the game as the kidnapped daughter of the president, but often helps Leon solve puzzles and gets her own separate section of the game when she gets separated from Leon.
Osmund Saddler
The main antagonist of Resident Evil 4 Remake. He is behind the Plagas parasites that are infecting the people of residential Spain where Ashley Graham is kidnapped and Leon Kennedy goes to save her.
