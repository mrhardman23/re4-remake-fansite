Resident Evil 4 Remake

A Fan Website for the Video Game, Resident Evil 4 Remake

Introduction

This is a fan website for the video game, Resident Evil 4 Remake.

Released by Capcom in 2023, the video game follows the story of Leon Kennedy as he attempts to save the kidnapped daughter of the President of the United States, Ashley Graham.

Who is Capcom?

"Capcom Co., Ltd. (Japanese: 株式会社カプコン, Hepburn: Kabushiki-gaisha Kapukon) is a Japanese video game company. It has created a number of critically acclaimed and multi-million-selling game franchises, with its most commercially successful being Resident Evil, Monster Hunter, Street Fighter, Mega Man, Devil May Cry, Onimusha, Dead Rising, Dragon's Dogma, Ace Attorney, and Marvel vs. Capcom. Established in 1979, it has become an international enterprise with subsidiaries in East Asia (Hong Kong), Europe (London, England), and North America (San Francisco, California)." -  Capcom Wikipedia Page

Location

The story is set in a fictional area of rural Spain where a mysterious cult has begun to convert the people who live in the area into something other than human.

Why is it a Remake?

Resident Evil 4 Remake is, at the time of writing, the most recent game in the Resident Evil series to receive the "remake" treatment with improved visuals, graphics, and gameplay based off the original Resident Evil 4 game, which was released in 2005.
